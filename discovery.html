1st Session - “KICK OFF”

This session consists in a presential meeting between two teams. Your team is built by you with whoever you want. Our advice is to built the most diversified team you can up to fifteen members. The other team, from our side, will gather your counterparts based on our specialists and on our partners’ network. We will work together starting from identifying up to 50 challenges you are facing, so then we can prioritize them. By the end of this session we will acknowledge yours 3 most valuable problems. We are now ready to find solutions...

2nd Session - “HxD”

In this sessions we will use an approach called Fujitsu Human Centric Experience Design - HxD. This approach will help us understand the challenges, its context on your strategy and where you stand from different perspectives. Inspiration cards, refined from real-world workshops, are introduced to inspire creativity, to encourage working at pace and to accelerate the formation of ideas. New ideas are captured on paper cards, digitalized and added to the solution's development. The output is a plan to address each challenge, with a detailed focus on the most important one. Nevertheless, all of your challenges will be addressed and analyzed. You will now have a clear view of where to go, what to do and who to do with.

We are now ready to find solutions...
