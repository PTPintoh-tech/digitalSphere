Proof of Concept (PoC)


A Proof of Concept (PoC) is a exercise to test the design of an idea or assumption

The main purpose of developing a POC is to demonstrate the functionality and to verify a certain concept or theory that can be achieved in development.

Prototype


Prototyping is a valuable exercise that allows the innovator to visualize how the product will function, it is a working interactive model of the end product that gives an idea of the design, navigation and layout.

Minimum Viable Product (MVP)


A Minimum Viable Product (MVP) is a development technique in which a new product is developed with sufficient features to satisfy early adopters.
